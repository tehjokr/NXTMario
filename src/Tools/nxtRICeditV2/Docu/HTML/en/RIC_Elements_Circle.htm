RIC elements

 

Circle
 

With RIC element circle you can draw a circle on the NXT screen. If some parts of the circle will be outside of the visible area, than the rest will drawn on the NXT screen.

Size of element: 12 Byte
   
Hint:

With the mouse you can define the center of the circle and also the radius of it. Pressing the mouse button on the working area will define the center and by releasing the mouse button you define the radius. Please keep in mind that a manual setting of the circle by using the mouse will set the parameters for center point and also for radius to constant values.
